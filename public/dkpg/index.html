Soccer Coaching – Firelands United

Firelands United FC

Adult men’s soccer club in Northern Ohio – Founded in 2023

Firelands United is a competitive soccer club based in the Firelands region of Northern Ohio. The club was founded in 2023 and competes in the Northern Ohio Soccer League (NOSL). The goal of the club is to grow the game locally, develop players, and build a strong soccer community across the region.

The team focuses on player development, competitive matches, and creating opportunities for local players to compete at a higher level without leaving the area. Firelands United also promotes community involvement and aims to bring players, fans, and families together through soccer.
